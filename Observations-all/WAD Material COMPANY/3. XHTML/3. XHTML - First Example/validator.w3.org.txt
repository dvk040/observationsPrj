How to check XHTML file valid or invalid using online tool:
------------------------------------------------------------

   1) Open browser. Ex: Mozilla Firefox

   2) Go to https://validator.w3.org/

   3) Click on "Validate by FileUpload".

   4) Click on "Browse".

   5) Select the html file. Ex: xhtml.html

   6) Click on "Check".

   7) It shows list of errors or "Passed" message.
